Core Features
That Drive Results

AI-Powered Scheduling

Streamline your entire appointment process with intelligent automation. Our AI scheduling system understands patient preferences, provider availability, and treatment complexity — ensuring every slot is optimized and conflicts are eliminated. Handle multi-appointment treatments effortlessly while improving provider utilization across your practice.

Smart Reminders & Confirmations

Keep patients engaged and on schedule with adaptive, behavior-based reminders. AI analyzes response patterns to send confirmations and reminders at the perfect time and channel — SMS, email, or app notifications. Reduce no-shows by 60% and maintain a consistent, predictable workflow.

Insurance Verification

Simplify billing and eligibility checks with real-time automation. Instantly verify patient insurance coverage, submit pre-authorization requests, and detect issues before treatment begins. Reduce administrative effort and claim denials by 40%, creating a seamless experience for both staff and patients.

Treatment Plan Communication

Deliver clear, personalized, and transparent treatment plans that build trust and confidence. Automatically share detailed explanations of procedures, cost estimates, and timelines through each patient's preferred communication channel. Increase treatment acceptance and improve financial clarity for every case.

Post-Treatment Follow-up

Extend patient care beyond the clinic with intelligent, automated follow-ups. Send recovery reminders, check healing progress, and gather satisfaction feedback through personalized messages. Strengthen long-term relationships and improve patient retention by 45% with effortless post-care management.

Practice Analytics Dashboard

Unlock real-time insights into every aspect of your practice. Monitor patient engagement, staff performance, and revenue trends from a single, intuitive dashboard. Track ROI, identify growth opportunities, and make confident, data-driven decisions powered by predictive analytics.
